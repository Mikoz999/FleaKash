Kenyan Account Activation

To complete your Activation follow the steps bellow;

  1. Go To M-Pesa menu
  2. Select Lipa na Mpesa
  3. Buy Goods and services
  4. Enter Buy Goods till number 12345
  5. Enter Mpesa pin
  6. Enter Amount 240
  7. Confirm payment

If you do not have an account create one
